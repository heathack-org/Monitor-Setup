Use this form to connect a thermal monitor to the public wifi on your premises.

Enter your WiFi SSID (network name) and password, but don't press submit.

Turn off mobile data and use your wifi settings to turn off auto-connection to any wifi networks that are in range.

Turn on the thermal monitor. In your wifi settings, look for a new network with a name that starts with "heathack". It may take up to a minute to appear. Connect to it. It only connects between your device and the monitor - it will not give you internet access.

Then press submit.
